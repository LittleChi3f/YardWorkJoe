About Yard Work Joe's

Yard Work Joe’s is a proudly locally owned and operated landscaping company based in Prescott, Arizona. Since our founding, we’ve grown from a small passion project into a trusted name for lawn care, landscape design, and outdoor maintenance in our community.

Our mission is simple: to help homeowners and businesses create beautiful, functional, and sustainable outdoor spaces that enhance their quality of life and property value.

Our experienced team specializes in everything from lawn care and seasonal clean-up to full-scale landscape design. We take pride in creating outdoor environments that are as beautiful as they are functional—spaces you’ll enjoy for years to come.

We believe in sustainable landscaping practices, clear communication, and showing up when we say we will. Whether you're a homeowner looking to refresh your yard or a property manager seeking ongoing maintenance, Yard Work Joe’s is here to help.

Your vision, our expertise. Let's build the yard of your dreams—starting today.

Meet the Owner

Joe is a certified landscape designer with over 15 years of hands-on experience in lawn care, horticulture, and outdoor design. He founded Yard Work Joe’s with a mission to offer professional, reliable, and affordable landscaping services to his hometown of Prescott.

Joe's approach is personal—he oversees every project, builds relationships with clients, and brings thoughtful design to every yard. His passion is rooted in helping people turn ordinary spaces into outdoor sanctuaries.

When he's not leading a landscaping crew, Joe can be found hiking local trails or volunteering at community beautification events. He believes that every yard deserves care, creativity, and a personal touch.
